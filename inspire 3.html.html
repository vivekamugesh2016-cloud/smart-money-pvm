Inspiring Stories

Indian students & teens who became money-smart early!

Advait Thakur

Age 12, Founder of Apex Infosys

Started coding at 6, founded Apex Infosys India at age 12. Built apps & websites – shows learning skills early can lead to big success!

"Start small, learn daily → become entrepreneur young."

Tilak Mehta

Age 13, Founder of Papers N Parcels

At 13, started Papers N Parcels – low-cost delivery service in Mumbai using dabbawalas. Now a successful teen business!

"Spot problems around you (like parcel delivery) and solve them smartly."

Sreelakshmi Suresh

Age 11, CEO of eDesign

Began web designing at 9, became CEO of eDesign at 11. Designed websites for big clients – youngest in world!

"Practice skills like coding/design → turn hobby into business."

Vinusha MK

Age 13, Founder of Four Seasons Pastry

Started Four Seasons Pastry at 13 – bakes & sells cupcakes, now offers baking kits. Built brand using social media!

"Use pocket money ideas + social media = real business."

"Age is just a number – smart habits & hard work make you rich in future!"

– Inspired for you, Hareesh from Tirunelveli!
